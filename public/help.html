Get started with notePull

To create a new note

1. Click on an existing folder (there is a default created for you already).
2. Click "+ NEW NOTE"
3. Click the "+" button on the bottom to add a new bullet.

To highlight/unhighlight a bullet

To highlight:
1. Click on the middle icon on the top toolbar to select a color.
2. Click the bullet you want highlighted.

To unhighlight:
1. On the top toolbar, click on the middle icon to select the clear color (on the very right).
2. Click the bullet you want unhighlighted.

To search

1. Click on "Search" from the home page.
2. Select the highlight color of bullets you want to filter.

Note: search filters through all your notes

To go to the home page

You can always go to the home page by clicking on the notePull icon located at the top of the page you are on.

To change your email/password

1. Click on "Settings" from the home page.
2. Type your new email and/or password twice and save.
